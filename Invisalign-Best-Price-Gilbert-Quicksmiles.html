Your Invisalign dentist will be familiar with five concerns that are common with their patients' teeth. These problems can all result in serious dental health problems and must be corrected where possible.

1. Overbite - When your upper teeth bite over your lower teeth;
2. Underbite - When your upper teeth bite under your lower teeth;
3. Extremely crowded teeth - When there is insufficient space in your jaw for your teeth to fit in typically;
4. Commonly spaced teeth - When there is too much area between your teeth;
5. Crossbite - When some of your upper teeth bite above your lower teeth, and some bite under your lower teeth.
