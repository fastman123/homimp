MDF ({Medium Density Fiber) Board:

Medium Density Fiber Board (MDF) is denser than fragment board as well as plywood. It is made with an even thickness all throughout and at the same time, is smoother on every side. This type of difficult board is made from wood fiber which is constantly heated and also glued under pressure.

• Applications:

- Panels as well as shutter doors
- Divider panel boards
- Cabinets
- Shelving
- Furnishings structure applications
- Paneling as well as indoor cladding
- Wooden invention products
- Decorative wall cladding

• Benefits:

- Good for veneers and overlays
- Approves water as well as oil based gloss as well as paints
- Can be machined effortlessly
- Can be recorded, machined, burnt out and also cut without damaging the surface area
- No wooden grain
- Very smooth, company, leveled and also thick surface
- Less expensive than plywood

• Shortcomings:

- Goggles and also covers should be exactly used while machining for fending off dust.
- Includes Urea Formaldehyde. Care must be taken while sanding and also cutting. Guide or paint can reduce continued out grassing (off-grassing).
- Can't be tarnished
- Extremely heavy in contrast with fragment board
- Does not hold screws perfectly, nonetheless is feasible while care is taken.
- Absorbs wetness unless secured appropriately
